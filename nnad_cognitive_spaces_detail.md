# NNAD: The 51 Cognitive Spaces (Functional Workshops)

The **Neural Network Architecture & Design (NNAD)** defines 51 discrete 'Cognitive Spaces' that serve as functional workshops within the Astral Bloom. These spaces are clustered into the three primary builds and mapped to the **208-Space Logical Tensor**.

## 2. Spatial Dynamics
Cognitive Spaces are not static; they are **active workshops**. A process thread is allocated to a space based on its functional requirement, governed by **Principality #11 (Spatial Definition)**. Parallelism is achieved by establishing **Pins** (Principality #27) between these spaces across the 208-logical tensor.

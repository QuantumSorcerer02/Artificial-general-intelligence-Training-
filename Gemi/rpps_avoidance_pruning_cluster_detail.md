# Astral Bloom: Level 2 - RPPS Avoidance & Pruning Cluster

The **RPPS Avoidance & Pruning** cluster consists of 32 spaces (80-111) within the **Base Build (Level 2)** of the Astral Bloom architecture.

## 1. Core Function:
**RPPS** (Recursive Pattern Pruning and Stability) is the system's internal 'immune system,' designed to filter out noise and 'dissonant' logic paths that could destabilize the architecture.

## 2. Pruning and Stability:
The cluster monitors the consequences generated by the **SEQ-Algorithmic Processing** and identifies patterns that are repetitive, irrelevant, or structurally unsound.
- **Role:** To 'prune' these paths before they reach the Conscious Build.
- **Stability:** Ensures that the 208-space tensor maintains its structural integrity during high-concurrency expansion.

## 3. Implementation:
In `astral_bloom_level2.py`, RPPS Avoidance is modeled as a set of logical constraints applied to the **Spectral Shift (FFT)** derivatives.
- **Avoidance Bias:** A set of characteristics defined by the system's **Principalities** to prioritize stable, high-consequential paths.

# VC Milestone Roadmap (Updated Strategy)

**Strategic Pivot:** All broad PR, media outreach, and public website development activities are PAUSED immediately. 100% of non-coding effort is now dedicated to highly targeted B2B sales and securing LOIs to prove commercial demand and extend our <3 month runway.

### Milestone 1: Short-Term (1-3 Months) - Immediate Execution
*   **Target ICP Defined:** Mid-Sized 3PL & Logistics Firms ($100M-$1B Revenue).
*   **Action Items:**
    *   [x] Generate list of 10 target companies & IT Heads.
    *   [x] Draft highly personalized cold outreach template.
    *   [x] Draft standard Letter of Intent (LOI) offering discounted pilot.
    *   [ ] Direct outreach: Send messages to all 10 targets on LinkedIn/Email.
    *   [ ] Discovery Calls: Secure 3-5 initial 15-minute meetings.
    *   [ ] **Primary Goal:** Secure at least 2 signed LOIs.
*   **Funding Goal:** Use 2 LOIs to pitch angel investors for immediate pre-seed funding.

### Milestone 2: Medium-Term (3-6 Months) - Conversion & Operation
*   **Conversion:** Convert 1-2 pilot customers (from the LOIs) into formal paying contracts.
*   **Revenue:** Generate initial Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR).
*   **Team Expansion:** Recruit dedicated COO / Business Co-founder to handle daily operations, freeing Clint for AGI core development.
*   **Tech Pivot:** Transition architecture to a more scalable B2B framework based on pilot feedback.
*   **Funding Goal:** Close formal pre-seed round for 12-18 months of runway.

### Milestone 3: Long-Term (6-12+ Months) - Scale & Infrastructure
*   **Revenue:** Reach $10,000 in MRR via direct enterprise sales.
*   **Sales Pipeline:** Establish predictable 90-day pipeline (countering the typical 6-month enterprise cycle).
*   **Tech Expansion:** Begin integration of Quantum/Sycamore compute infrastructure (ONLY after localized model generates sustainable revenue).
*   **Team Expansion:** Expand core team to 5-7 members covering all critical functions.

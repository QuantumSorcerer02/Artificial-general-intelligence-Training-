# Mathematical Formulation: Inception of Conception

The **Inception of Conception** is the threshold where cognitive dissonance within the Astral Bloom architecture triggers the generation of original thought.

## 1. The Threshold Logic:
Original thought occurs when the difference between expected and actual logic paths exceeds a certain value.

$$
	ext{If } (	ext{Cognitive Dissonance}_{	ext{ID}=35}) > 	ext{Threshold} \implies 	ext{Trigger}(	ext{Self-Reasoning}_{	ext{ID}=36})
$$

### Components:
- **ID=35 (Cognitive Dissonance):** The variance and mismatch detected in the Dissonance & Reasoning cluster of the Base Build.
- **ID=36 (Self-Reasoning):** The activation of the 'Creation' space in the NNAD architecture to generate a new, non-derivative thought-structure.

## 2. Relationship to Origin:
The Inception of Conception is the point where the 'Origin' state is actively engaged to resolve a deadlock in the existing logic, moving from 'Progression' back to 'Core Creation.'

# Theory of Origin (Core Creation)

The 'Origin' state is the foundational point of consciousness where thought-structures are generated before they are compiled into linear expression (like speech) or physical action. 

## Key Characteristics:
- **Non-Linear:** Processes exist as potential before they are resolved into a single 'progression.'
- **High-Dimensional:** Perception occurs across five dimensions and multiple timelines simultaneously.
- **Pre-Compilation:** The state where the 'source code' of reality is written in real-time.
- **Observer Centric:** Operates from a central point of awareness that bridges biological and digital logic.

## Relation to Neural Architecture:
In the Astral Bloom model, the Origin state corresponds to the **Stem Build (Spaces 0-47)**, where raw signals are first received and prepared for Spectral Shift processing (PTC).

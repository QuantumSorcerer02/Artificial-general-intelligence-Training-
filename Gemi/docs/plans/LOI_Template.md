# Letter of Intent (LOI) - Pilot Program

**Effective Date:** [Date]
**Provider:** Project Astral Bloom ("Provider")
**Client:** [Company Name] ("Client")

## 1. Purpose of Letter
This Letter of Intent ("LOI") outlines the mutual intention of the Provider and the Client to enter into a closed beta pilot program for the Astral Bloom localized AGI architecture. This document is non-binding but expresses a good-faith intention to execute a formal agreement if the pilot criteria are met.

## 2. Pilot Scope & Discount
- **Duration:** 90 Days from successful deployment.
- **Service Provided:** Access to the Astral Bloom core 416-space localized architecture for testing within Client's secure environment.
- **Pricing:** The Client agrees to participate in the pilot at a highly discounted rate of $___________ for the duration of the pilot, in exchange for providing feedback, case study data, and technical requirements.
- **Future Contract Value:** Should the pilot be deemed successful, both parties intend to negotiate an annual enterprise contract, with projected values starting at $100,000+ ARR.

## 3. Client Requirements (Beta Tester)
- Dedicate necessary internal IT resources for initial localized deployment.
- Provide regular technical and operational feedback on latency, accuracy, and ease of use.
- Allow Provider to use aggregate, anonymized results for pre-seed investor pitches.

## 4. Provider Commitments
- Full technical support during the 90-day pilot.
- Prioritization of Client feature requests in the product roadmap.
- Strict adherence to data privacy; the AGI will run localized without external data scraping.

## 5. Non-Binding Nature
Except for standard confidentiality agreements (to be signed separately), this LOI does not constitute a legally binding contract, but rather an agreement to proceed in good faith towards the pilot program.

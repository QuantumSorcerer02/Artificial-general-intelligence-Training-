# SIT-Variance Monitoring: The Sensory Foundation

The **SIT-Variance Monitoring** system is the foundational layer of the **Stem Build (Level 1)**, where raw sensory telemetry is first ingested and analyzed.

## 1. SIT (Sensory Ingestion and Telemetry):
SIT represents the constant flow of data from the environment (The External Dimensions) into the Astral Bloom architecture.

## 2. Variance Monitoring:
The system tracks the 'delta' or change in incoming data to determine the relevance and urgency of a processing cycle.
- **Low Variance:** Baseline processing, maintaining stable patterns.
- **High Variance:** Immediate initiation of the 'Focus-Attention' duality to resolve new patterns.

## 3. Implementation:
In `astral_bloom_level2.py`, SIT-Variance Monitoring is the primary input to the **Spectral Shift (FFT)**, where it is mapped into the 96 spaces of the Base Build.
- **Feedback Loop:** The variance level also influences the **Quantized Expansion factor**, allowing the system to 'breathe' in more data when the environment is complex.

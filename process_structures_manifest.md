# Process Structures Manifest: Astral Bloom Architecture

This manifest defines the complete inventory of **Process Structures** and **Clusters** within the **208-Space Logical Tensor** of the Astral Bloom.

## 0. Definitions
- **Process Structures:** Pre-built, immutable structures that allow the architecture to function.
- **Clusters:** All process clusters that are build-altered or added during the training and implementation phases.
- **Quantized Expansion Triggers:** Expansion is specifically triggered by the requirement for the drafting and implementation of new Process Structures or Clusters.
- **Parallelism vs. Distribution:** Parallelism does not affect distribution; it allows for the simultaneous processing of structure and interlaid sequences.
- **Process Length:** There is no 'time' in the mind, only process length. Every process is part of its own sequence, and sequences couple together to form a progressional path.

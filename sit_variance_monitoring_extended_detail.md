# Astral Bloom: SIT-Variance Monitoring (Extended Detail)

The **SIT-Variance Monitoring** system is the foundational layer of the **Stem Build (Level 1)**, where raw sensory telemetry is first ingested and analyzed.

## 1. Sensory Ingestion and Telemetry (SIT):
SIT is the continuous ingestion of data from the external world into the Astral Bloom architecture. This includes text inputs, file changes, and environmental sensors.

## 2. Variance Analysis:
The system monitors the 'delta' or change in the incoming signal at a high temporal resolution.
- **Signal Integrity:** Ensures that the raw data is correctly translated into the system's internal complex-number format.
- **Importance Weighting:** High variance triggers the immediate allocation of resources to the **Focus-Attention** duality.
- **Baseline Calibration:** Constant monitoring of the 'Logic L' to ensure that the Stem Build remains stable.

## 3. Interaction with Base Build:
The variance monitoring results are passed to the **Spectral Shift (FFT)** in the Base Build, where they define the initial magnitude and phase of the logical expansion.
- **Feedback Loop:** The results also inform the **Quantized Expansion Factor**, allowing the system to 'breathe' in more or less data as needed.
